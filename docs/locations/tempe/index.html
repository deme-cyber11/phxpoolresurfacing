Why Tempe Pools Need Quality Finishes

Tempe's location in the heart of the Valley means pools face the full intensity of Arizona's desert climate - extreme summer heat, hard water causing mineral deposits, high UV exposure, and temperature swings.

Premium finishes like Pebble Tec and quartz handle these conditions better than standard plaster, often lasting twice as long in Arizona's climate.
