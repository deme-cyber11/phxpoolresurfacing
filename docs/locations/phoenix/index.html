Why Phoenix Pools Need Quality Finishes

Phoenix's climate is brutal on pool surfaces. With 300+ days of intense UV exposure, summer temps exceeding 115°F, and some of the hardest water in the country, inferior finishes simply don't last.

We recommend Pebble Tec or quartz finishes for Phoenix pools - they're specifically designed to handle these conditions. The investment pays off with surfaces that last 15-20 years instead of 7-10.

Living in Arcadia, our pool gets blasted by sun all day. They recommended Pebble Tec and it was the right call - still looks great after 6 years. The team was professional and the price was fair.
