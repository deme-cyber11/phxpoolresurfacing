What is Quartz Finish?

Quartz pool finishes combine crushed quartz crystals with white cement, creating a stunning surface that sparkles in the sunlight. The crystals catch light beautifully while providing better durability than standard plaster.

It's the perfect middle ground - more affordable than Pebble Tec, more durable than plaster, and smoother than aggregate finishes.
