Waterline Tile Replacement

The waterline tile around your pool takes a beating from sun, chemicals, and water level changes. Cracked, faded, or missing tiles hurt your pool's appearance and can let water seep behind.

We offer ceramic, porcelain, glass, and stone tile options to complement any pool finish.

Coping Stone Repair

Coping stones form the edge where your deck meets the pool. Over time, they crack, shift, or become loose - both unsightly and potentially dangerous.

Options include concrete, travertine, brick, and decorative pavers to match your style.
