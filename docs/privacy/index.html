Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Property address and pool details for estimate purposes
  • Messages you send through our contact form

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide pool resurfacing estimates
  • Schedule and perform pool resurfacing services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only when necessary to provide our services (e.g., with subcontractors working on your project).

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This service may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted securely through their service. Please review Web3Forms' privacy policy for details on their data handling practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to secure data transmission.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at contact@phxpoolresurfacing.com or calling (602) 693-0112.
