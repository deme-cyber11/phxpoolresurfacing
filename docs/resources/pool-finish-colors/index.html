Best Pool Finish Colors for Arizona Homes

How to choose the perfect pool color for Phoenix's desert climate

Choosing the right pool finish color transforms your backyard. In Arizona, color choice affects more than aesthetics - it impacts water temperature, maintenance, and how well stains show.

Popular Pool Colors in Phoenix

White & Light Blue (Classic)

The timeless choice that makes water appear bright blue. Best for:

  • Traditional home styles
  • Pools with significant shade
  • Homeowners who want the "resort" look

Consideration: Light colors show staining more easily in Arizona's hard water.

Medium Blue & Aqua

A great middle-ground that hides minor stains while maintaining a vibrant appearance. Popular options include:

  • Pebble Tec Ocean Blue
  • Quartz Crystal Blue
  • NPP Blue Lagoon

Desert Sand & Tan

Increasingly popular in Arizona for good reason:

  • Hides calcium deposits and staining
  • Complements desert landscaping
  • Creates a natural lagoon appearance
  • Lower maintenance in hard water areas

Dark Blue & Midnight

Creates dramatic depth and a sophisticated look. Considerations:

  • Water appears deeper and darker
  • Absorbs more heat (warmer water)
  • Excellent at hiding stains
  • Best for pools without heavy sun exposure

How Color Affects Water Temperature

In Phoenix's intense sun, darker finishes can increase water temperature by 5-10 degrees. This can be a pro or con:

  • Pro: Extended swimming season in spring/fall
  • Con: Pool may be too warm in peak summer

If temperature control is important, lighter colors with a pool cooler or darker colors with shade structures work well.
